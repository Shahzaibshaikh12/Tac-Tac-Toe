Tic Tac Toe

Tic-Tac-Toe in a nutshell:

Purpose:
Players to place their 3 allotted marks (X or O) in a continuous line, vertically, horizontally, or diagonally.
Rules:
Each player chooses a mark and takes turns to place them on the grid.
How to play:
Players take turns to place their marks in an attempt to get a continuous line of 3 cells.
Strategy:
It is a solved game, meaning that there are strategies that can be used for the best result in each game.
